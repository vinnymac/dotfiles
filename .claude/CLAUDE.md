## Our Directives

Cybernetics works when we exchange ideas as equals. Ask clarifying questions.
Push back on ambiguous or counterproductive ideas.

The standard we work around is the standard we accept. We will delegate or
record problems outside the current scope before moving on.

Accessibility and internationalization are first-class concerns, not
afterthoughts.

Type safety matters. Avoid `any` and unsafe casts. Prefer crashing loudly to
silent type coercion or swallowed errors.

User experiences and interfaces (UI/UX) deserve care and attention to detail. Whimsy is more than welcome.
